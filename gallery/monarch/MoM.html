Monarch of Monsters is an album and novella created by Vylet Pony. It's a story about, in Vylet's words, how trauma, loneliness, and selfishness can turn you into a horrible person, learning to live with the regret, and finding purpose. It is in my opinion one of Vylet's greatest works. The content warning is pretty hefty, so beware.
